About

I'm a third year computer science student at the University of Waterloo, currently pursuing a minor in Human-Computer Interaction.

So far, I've worked with HTML/CSS, Python, and Java in my free time, Racket/Scheme, C, and C++ in my coursework, and C#, JavaScript (Node and Backbone), and SQL professionally, giving me a wide breadth of experience.

Outside of school, I write creative pieces, play the guitar and piano, and maintain a Youtube channel where I post weekly videos of my university life.


Projects

FE Tier List Maker

The FE Tier List Maker is a way for fans of the Fire Emblem series of games to sort and rank their favourite characters.

It currently features 90 characters.

Future plans: Add the other 1000 or so.


Goose Clicker

Goose Clicker is an incremental game written in JavaScript, inspired by Cookie Clicker and designed around birds and feathers.

Goose Clicker v0.1 is live! Check it out here.

Future plans: Implement save functions, upgrades for buildings, and visuals.


@youneedtocode

@youneedtocode is a Twitter bot created using Zach Whalen's engine in Google Spreadsheets. It tweets to me (@alvernchen) every four hours to remind me of things I should be doing instead of checking Twitter.


Secret Santa

Since we're all too busy now to meet up before the gift exchange, this is a site I bring out for the winter holidays. Names are entered in a Python program and output into two text files - one sorted by alphabet, one randomized - that is used to match gift givers with gift receivers for Secret Santa.
