Projects

Goose Clicker

Goose Clicker is an incremental game in the style of Cookie Clicker designed around the theme of Canada geese and other Canadian cultural icons.

Goose Clicker v0.1 is live! Check it out here.

Future plans: Implement save functions, upgrades for buildings, and visuals.


@youneedtocode

@youneedtocode is a Twitter bot created using Zach Whalen's engine in Google Spreadsheets. It tweets to me (@alvernchen) every four hours to remind me to live life to the fullest.

Future plans: Develop my own code for tweeting from a spreadsheet, with the ability to construct tweets by cell as well as tweet an entire cell and nothing else at random.


Breathe

Breathe is an incremental game in progress, with the objective of bringing mental health issues and the importance of self-expression to light.
