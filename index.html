About

Hey, my name's Alvern. I'm a computer science undergrad from the University of Waterloo.

I've primarily coded in Dart, C#, SQL, JavaScript (Node, Backbone, and jQuery), and Java, and I've deployed in Azure and AWS over my years of internships and full-time experience.

Through those, I've built desktop and web apps, delivered mobile app features, architected a CI/CD pipeline, and written way, way too many test suites.

In my free time, I like to write short stories and comics, play the guitar and piano, and maintain a Youtube channel where I posted regular videos of my university life from when I moved into residence in 2016 until I graduated (remotely) in 2021.
